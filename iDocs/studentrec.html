Brief Project Summary

Student Recommendation System

The student recommendation is meant to help guide students in making career decisions based on their talents and aptitudes and informed by industrial trends. It is meant to guide the students from when they are in primary school all the way to university. And in this way it will increase satisfaction with careers while also preventing saturation of particular career choices.

>

Literature Review

The search for a curriculum that enables students to achieve their very best and thus improves the quality of life for future adults has been going on for several years. UNESCO coined the term Competency Based Curriculum. The competency based curriculum is meant to emphasize on the complex outcomes of a learning process (i.e. knowledge skills and attitudes to be applied by learners). Several countries such as South Africa, Kenya and Sweden have all adopted some form of CBC all to varying degrees of success

In Kenya it has been noted that shortage of teachers and poor infrastructure are the main limitations facing the adoption of the CBC in Kenya.

We aim to solve this problem by using a recommendation engine similar to those used by entertainment services such as YouTube and Spotify to solve this problem.

Problem Statement

Often times students are not well informed about possible career paths during their high school education. This is due to a number of facts including but not limited to:

  1. Lack of exposure: Due to financial constraints many students do not know where they may best apply their skills and thus end up taking courses that do not push them to farther their talents
  2. Outdated advice from teachers: Since a bunch of teachers have not had the opportunity to interact with industry professionals the inforamtion they give their students may be out of date and thus might actually mislead the students.
